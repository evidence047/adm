About the University

Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST) is a public university located in Bondo, Kenya. It offers diverse academic programs focusing on science, technology, and community development.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Innovation and Research
  • Community Service

Key Faculties

  • School of Science
  • School of Engineering and Technology
  • School of Education
  • School of Business and Economics
