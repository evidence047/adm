Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University

Welcome to our notes about Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST).

About the University

JOOUST is a public university located in Bondo, Kenya, offering diverse academic programs focusing on science, technology, and community development.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Innovation and Research
  • Community Service

Key Faculties

  • School of Science
  • School of Engineering and Technology
  • School of Education
  • School of Business and Economics

Student Life

The university provides vibrant student life with clubs, societies, sports, and co-curricular activities to enrich personal growth and leadership skills.

Research & Innovation

JOOUST encourages research and innovation across all faculties, fostering solutions for local and global challenges.

Community Engagement

Community service is integral, with students participating in outreach programs and projects benefiting the wider society.
